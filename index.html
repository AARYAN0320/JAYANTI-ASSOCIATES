Architectural Solutions & Interior Design Studio

Jayanti Associates is a multidisciplinary architecture and interior design firm led by Ar. Chitwan Bamotra and Ar. Srishti Bamotra. The firm specializes in residential, commercial, interior and turnkey projects, delivering innovative design solutions with a strong focus on functionality, aesthetics and client satisfaction.

We manage interior design-build for offices, fit-outs, retail spaces, apartments, villas, sample flats and clubhouses. Our design/build service eliminates the client's burden of coordinating between different stakeholders — delivering affordable, yet contemporary designs from scratch, with a true turn-key approach.
